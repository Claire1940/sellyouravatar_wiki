<Section icon={Target} title="Steps to Handle Missing Items or Errors">

If you have completed a purchase but the item does not appear in your inventory, do not panic. This is often a synchronization issue rather than a failed transaction. Follow these specific steps to troubleshoot the issue before attempting to contact support.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Transaction History">
    Log into the Roblox website and view your "My Transactions" page. Confirm that the Robux were actually deducted and the item name is listed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Wait for Server Sync">
    Roblox states that during high-traffic periods, it can take anywhere from one hour to **24 hours** for a purchased asset to propagate through the global inventory system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use the Redraw Button">
    In the Avatar Editor, use the "Redraw" or "Refresh" option. This forces the system to re-scan your owned assets and can often make a "missing" item appear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify Inventory Category">
    Ensure you are looking in the correct category. A "Hat" will not show up in the "Face Accessory" section. Double-check the item type on the original listing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Issue | Likely Cause | Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Robux Deducted, No Item** | Server Lag | Wait 24 hours / Refresh |
| **Item Disappeared Later** | Moderation Removal | Contact Roblox Support |
| **Wrong Item Received** | Misleading Booth | Review Transaction History |
| **Purchase Failed** | Connectivity Issue | Check Internet / Re-login |
