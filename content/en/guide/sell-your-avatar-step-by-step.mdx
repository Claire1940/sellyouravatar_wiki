<Section icon={Target} title="Native Roblox Method: Step-by-Step Publishing">

The native method allows you to publish your avatar outfits directly from the Roblox avatar customization menu. This feature is highly integrated but generally requires an active subscription or specific developer permissions to access the publishing utility.

To begin this process, you must first prepare a custom asset. You can design classic clothing templates or submit 3D accessories through the official Roblox UGC program. Once your custom item is approved and available in your inventory, you can proceed with the setup.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Your Custom Asset">
    Open the Roblox avatar editor. Equip the custom shirt, T-shirt, or UGC accessory you designed. This asset acts as the primary monetization driver for your published outfit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Publishing Tool">
    Locate the plus sign icon or the publish option within your avatar customization dashboard. If you have an active Premium membership, this tool should be visible on your screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Configure Outfit Metadata">
    Enter a descriptive name for your outfit and write a detailed description. Use relevant keywords to help other players discover your design in the public directory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Publish and Verify">
    Submit the outfit to the public catalog. Navigate to the try-on section of your profile to confirm that your custom item is correctly linked and priced.
  </Step>
</Steps>
