<Section icon={Trophy} title="Native Publishing: Premium Avatar Selling">

If you hold an active Roblox Premium membership, you can publish your outfits directly from your inventory. This method integrates your designs into the official Roblox avatar marketplace, making them visible to players browsing profiles and community outfits. The primary benefit of this method is the professional presentation and the direct connection to your creator group or personal inventory.

To ensure your outfit is ready for sale, you must first design it within the Roblox Avatar Editor. Make sure your custom shirt or UGC item is equipped. According to the [Roblox Creator Hub Guidelines (2026 Update)](https://create.roblox.com/), uploading a classic shirt or pants costs a baseline fee of 10 Robux, which must be paid before you can list the item as part of your avatar outfit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Custom Assets">
    Open the Roblox Avatar Editor and equip your custom-created shirt, T-shirt, or UGC accessory alongside your chosen outfit combination.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Publish Menu">
    Locate the "+" sign on your avatar customization page. If this button is missing, verify that your Premium subscription is active and your account settings allow public inventory sharing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Define Outfit Details">
    Name your outfit with descriptive keywords and write a brief description highlighting the style. Avoid spamming tags, as this can trigger moderation filters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm and List">
    Click the publish button to list the outfit. The system will automatically calculate the total Robux cost of all items in the outfit and display it to potential buyers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When players browse your profile, they can view your published outfits, try them on, and purchase the entire bundle. The Robux generated from your custom items will deposit into your account after the standard pending transaction hold period, which typically lasts between three to seven days.

<Section icon={Map} title="Alternative Routes: Catalog Avatar Creator Guide">

For players who do not have a Premium subscription, the popular Roblox experience *Catalog Avatar Creator* offers a powerful alternative. This game allows users to design outfits using the entire Roblox catalog and publish them to a community feed. When other players purchase items through your published outfit inside the game, you earn an affiliate commission.

This method is highly accessible and has become the preferred route for casual designers. According to the [Catalog Avatar Creator Community Wiki (2026)](https://www.roblox.com/games/7041939546/Catalog-Avatar-Creator), creators receive a 10% commission on any catalog item purchased through their community outfit board. This commission applies even if you did not design the individual items yourself, making it a viable way to earn Robux through curation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter the Experience">
    Launch the Catalog Avatar Creator game on Roblox and open the main customization menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assemble and Save">
    Create your outfit using any combination of accessories, clothing, and body packages. Save the look to your local in-game slot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Publish to Community">
    Navigate to the "Community Outfits" section, select your saved outfit, and click the "Publish" button.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Distribute Outfit Code">
    Once published, the game generates a unique outfit code. Copy this code and share it on social media platforms to direct buyers to your design.
  </Step>
</Steps>

By sharing your outfit codes on platforms like Discord, TikTok, or YouTube, you can drive traffic directly to your creations. When users load your code in-game, they can purchase the entire set with a single click, earning you a steady stream of affiliate Robux.
